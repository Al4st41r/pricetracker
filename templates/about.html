About PriceTracker

Track prices of online items and get notified when they go on sale.

How It Works

PriceTracker helps you monitor product prices from online stores and alerts you when prices drop to your target.

How to Use

  1. Track a New Item: Paste the URL of the product page into the input field on the main page and click "Track New Item".
  2. Select the Price: The webpage will be loaded in an inspector view. Click on the price of the item you want to track.
  3. Confirm Details: A dialog will appear asking you to confirm the product name and enter your target price.
  4. View Tracked Items: The item will be added to your tracked items list on the main page.
  5. Get Notified: The application checks prices daily. If an item's price drops to or below your target, it will be highlighted in the list.
Pro Tip

Use the "Show only items on sale" filter to quickly see which tracked items have hit your target price.
